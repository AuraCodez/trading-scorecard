Trading Scorecard

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

Trading Scorecard is a browser extension that helps you track whether you followed your own trading rules. It does not collect, transmit, or sell your data.

Everything you enter — your rules, daily checklists, trade log (ticker, trade type, profit/loss, and notes), scores, and streak history — is stored locally on your own device using your browser's built-in extension storage. This data never leaves your device, is never sent to the developer or any third party, and is never used for advertising, analytics, or any purpose other than running the extension for you.

The extension requests only three permissions: storage (to save your scorecard on your device), alarms (to schedule a reminder at the U.S. market close), and notifications (to show that end-of-day summary). None of these transmit your data anywhere.

You can delete all of your data at any time by clearing the extension's storage or by removing the extension from your browser.
